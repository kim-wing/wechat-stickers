# Emotion Presets

Use these presets when the user gives only a character or broad theme. Replace any weak or off-theme item with a better chat scenario.

## 8-Pack Everyday

1. happy greeting - keyword: hi
2. received / understood - keyword: ok
3. thank you - keyword: thanks
4. sorry - keyword: sorry
5. cheering - keyword: cheer
6. tired / lying flat - keyword: tired
7. shocked - keyword: shock
8. goodbye - keyword: bye

Default animated motion for text sticker packs: use `micro_expression`, `head_only`, or `single_limb`. Keep the caption, body, feet, tail, and props locked unless the user asks for full-body motion.

Safe motion examples:

- greeting: one paw raises slightly, body locked
- received / understood: small blink or tiny nod, body locked
- thank you: eyes close and mouth smiles, body locked
- sorry: head dips a little, feet locked
- cheering: one paw lift or one sparkle, body locked
- tired: tiny sigh mouth change, body locked
- shocked: pupils and mouth change, body locked
- goodbye: one paw wave only, body locked

## Chinese Keyword Guidance

When preparing final metadata, write short Chinese meaning keywords such as:

- `开心`
- `收到`
- `谢谢`
- `抱歉`
- `加油`
- `震惊`
- `疑惑`
- `生气`
- `累了`
- `晚安`

Keep each keyword at 4 Chinese characters or fewer and avoid duplicates.
